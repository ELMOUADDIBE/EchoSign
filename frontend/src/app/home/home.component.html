About EchoSign

Learn it with us

EchoSign is dedicated to bridging the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf communities. We provide innovative solutions to translate Moroccan Sign Language (MSL) into text and audio, and vice versa. Our mission is to foster inclusivity, understanding, and learning.
